Best of God’s Own Country

Kerala, the Southernmost state of India is the spice hotspot of the world since ancient times. Kerala is a state which is a biodiversity hotspot in Incredible India. This beautiful land is rightly called God's Own Country, given to its lush green landscapes and crystal clear beaches that will leave you awestruck.

Know More!

Allappey

Alappuzha is a city on the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's best known for houseboat cruises.

Ernakulam

Ernakulam is known for Marine Drive, a busy waterfront promenade where boats offer backwater cruises.

Idukki

Idukki district is a densely forested, mountainous region in the south Indian state of Kerala.

Kannur

Kannur is one of the many beautiful places in Kerala that you must visit. Serene beaches, historic forts, folk art.

Kasaragod

The northernmost district of Kerala, Kasaragod is famous for its handlooms and its 293 kilometre long coastline.

Kollam

Kollam is a city in the state of Kerala, on India's Malabar Coast. It’s known as a trade hub and for its beaches.

Kottayam

The city is an important trading center of spices and commercial crops, especially rubber.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode is one of the best cities in Kerala. Endowed with lush green countrysides, serene beaches.

Malappuram

Malappuram has made numerous contributions to the cultural heritage of Kerala.

Palakkad

Palakkad is a vast stretch of fertile plains interspersed with hills, rivers, mountain streams and forests.

Pathanamthitta

Situated near the Western Ghats and bordered by the hills, famous for its scenic beauty, fairs and festivals

Thiruvananthapuram

It is a major tourist centre, known for the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, the beaches of Kovalam and Varkala.

thrissur

It is the land of Poorams. The district is known for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques.

Wayanad

This popular town is famous for wildlife and its spice plantations. Wayanad is part of a forest reserve.
